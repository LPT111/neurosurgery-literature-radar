来自工程化 BMSC 的细胞外囊泡通过传递 PBX1 改善骨关节炎小鼠受损的软骨。

Extracellular vesicles derived from engineered BMSCs improve damaged cartilage in mice with osteoarthritis by delivering PBX1.

Stem cell research & therapy｜2026-07-10

PMID: 42432728 ｜ DOI: 10.1186/s13287-026-05152-9

打开 PubMed / 原文

中文摘要

背景：骨髓间充质干细胞（BMSC）的有益作用与其分泌的细胞外囊泡（EV）有关。这些 EV 可以抑制软骨退化并防止关节细胞凋亡。我们的目的是探索工程化 BMSCs-EV 在治疗骨关节炎 (OA) 中的疗效。方法：通过超速离心技术分离 BMSC 衍生的 EV，然后进行表征。通过将软骨细胞亲和肽 (CAP) 和前 B 细胞白血病转录因子 1 (PBX1) 表达质粒转染至 BMSC 中获得工程化 EV。采用IL-1β处理软骨细胞诱导OA损伤，并通过DMM手术建立OA小鼠模型。在体外和体内检查了不同 EV 的功效。 EVs 治疗和联合遗传干预在体外和体内进行。结果：CAP 修饰和 PBX1 蛋白负载量的增加均增强了 EV 对软骨细胞的保护作用，并改善了 OA 引起的软骨损伤。 PBX1通过激活钙通道电压依赖性亚基β4（CACNB4）的转录表达来促进细胞外信号调节激酶（ERK）信号传导以及软骨细胞中下游分子c-FOS的表达。在 IL-1β 处理的软骨细胞以及 DMM 诱导的小鼠模型中，通过抑制 CACNB4/ERK 信号传导，工程化 EV 的软骨保护作用显着减弱。结论：工程化（CAP 修饰和增加 PBX1 蛋白负载）增强了 BMSCs-EV 对软骨细胞的治疗功效，这取决于 CACNB4/ERK 信号传导的激活。工程电动汽车是治疗 OA 软骨损伤的一种有前途的治疗方法。

英文摘要

BACKGROUND: The beneficial effects of bone marrow mesenchymal stem cells (BMSCs) have been linked to their secreted extracellular vesicles (EVs). These EVs can suppress cartilage degradation and prevent apoptosis in articular cells. We aimed to explore the therapeutic efficacy of engineered BMSCs-EVs in the treatment of osteoarthritis (OA). METHODS: The isolation of BMSC-derived EVs was conducted via ultracentrifugation techniques, followed by characterization. Engineered EVs were obtained by transfecting chondrocyte-affinity peptide (CAP) and Pre-B-cell leukemia transcription factor 1 (PBX1) expression plasmids into BMSCs. OA injury was induced by the treatment of chondrocytes with IL-1β, and the OA mouse model was established by DMM surgery. The efficacy of different EVs was examined in vitro and in vivo. EVs treatment and combined genetic interventions were performed in vitro and in vivo. RESULTS: Both CAP modification and increased protein loading of PBX1 strengthened the protective effect of EVs on chondrocytes and ameliorated OA-induced cartilage damage. PBX1 promoted extracellular signal-regulated kinase (ERK) signaling and the expression of the downstream molecule c-FOS in chondrocytes by activating the transcriptional expression of calcium channel voltage-dependent subunit beta 4 (CACNB4). The chondroprotective effect of engineered EVs was significantly attenuated by inhibiting CACNB4/ERK signaling in IL-1β-treated chondrocytes, as well as in the DMM-induced mouse model. CONCLUSION: Engineering (CAP modification and increased PBX1 protein loading) enhances the therapeutic efficacy of BMSCs-EVs on chondrocytes, which depends on the activation of the CACNB4/ERK signaling. Engineered EVs represent a promising treatment for OA cartilage damage.

中文摘要

背景：神经胶质细胞是大脑的非兴奋性细胞，在过度刺激时会引起神经精神疾病。最近的证据表明，它们是高度反应性的细胞，这使得它们容易受到环境刺激和生命早期的压力源的影响。当暴露于压力源而受到过度或慢性刺激时，这些细胞就会造成神经损伤，从而导致精神分裂症等神经精神疾病，这是一种没有明确干预措施的全球性负担。目的：蛋白质营养不良、免疫紊乱、毒素、父母分离和虐待等围产期应激源在对神经元和神经胶质细胞（星形胶质细胞、小胶质细胞和少突胶质细胞）的细胞结构和稳态产生负面影响方面发挥着重要作用，最终降低认知和行为能力。神经胶质过度激活和/或变性的影响导致成人个体出现神经炎症、记忆丧失、焦虑、抑郁和多动样症状，从而导致精神分裂症病理学的表现。结论：因此，人们相信，与围产期应激源相关的神经胶质细胞的负面变化可能会使个体在以后的生活中容易患上这种疾病。这篇综述总结了关于不同的早期生活压力源如何影响神经胶质细胞的当前证据，这可能有助于精神分裂症的神经生物学机制。

英文摘要

BACKGROUND: Glia are the non-excitable cells of the brain, which, upon hyperstimulation, give rise to neuropsychiatric disorders. Recent evidence suggests that they are highly reactive cells, which makes them prone to environmental stimuli and early-life stressors. Upon excessive or chronic stimulation through exposure to stressors, these cells become responsible for causing neurological damage, which leads to neuropsychiatric disorders like schizophrenia, a global burden with no definite interventions. PURPOSE: Perinatal stressors such as protein malnourishment, immunological disturbances, toxins, parental separation and abuse play a major role in negatively changing the cytoarchitecture and homeostasis of both neurons and glial cells (astrocytes, microglia and oligodendrocytes), finally degrading both cognitive and behavioural abilities. Effects of excessive glial activation and/or degeneration result in neuroinflammation, memory loss, anxiety, depression and hyperactivity-like symptoms in adult individuals, which contribute to the manifestation of schizophrenic pathology. CONCLUSION: Therefore, it is believed that perinatal stressor-associated negative changes in glia can predispose an individual to develop this disorder later in life. This review summarises current evidence on how diverse early-life stressors influence glial cells, which could contribute to the neurobiological mechanism underlying schizophrenia.

中文摘要

目的：睡眠障碍在创伤性脑损伤（TBI）后很常见，并且由于药物副作用和复杂的神经精神合并症而难以治疗。无创电前庭系统刺激 (VSS) 是一种新兴的神经调节疗法，已证明对无已知脑损伤的成人原发性慢性失眠有益，但尚未描述对 TBI 个体的慢性失眠有疗效。结果：我们提出了一项回顾性病例系列，其中包括 5 名患有慢性 TBI 和中度至重度失眠（失眠严重指数 [ISI] ≥ 15）的成年退伍军人，他们接受了每晚家庭 VSS 治疗。所有患者在 3 至 8 周的随访中均报告主观睡眠改善。 ISI 评分从基线时的 25.0 ± 2.5（平均值 ± 标准差）下降至随访时的 7.2 ± 4.7，减少了 17.8 ± 6.6 分。每位患者均表现出具有临床意义的 ISI 降低（≥6 点降低）。一些人报告噩梦减少，白天警觉性提高。结论：在本病例系列中，VSS 的使用与慢性 TBI 退伍军人慢性失眠严重程度的临床有意义的降低相关。

英文摘要

OBJECTIVE: Sleep dysfunction is common after traumatic brain injury (TBI) and can be difficult to manage due to medication side effects and complex neuropsychiatric comorbidities. Noninvasive electrical vestibular system stimulation (VSS) is an emerging neuromodulation therapy that has demonstrated benefit for primary chronic insomnia in adults without known brain injury, but has not been described for chronic insomnia in individuals with TBI. RESULTS: We present a retrospective case series of 5 adult veterans with chronic TBI and moderate-to-severe insomnia (Insomnia Severity Index [ISI] ≥15) who were treated with nightly home VSS. All patients reported subjective improvement in sleep at 3 to 8 week follow-up. ISI score decreased from 25.0 ± 2.5 (mean ± SD) at baseline to 7.2 ± 4.7 at follow-up, representing a reduction of 17.8 ± 6.6 points. Each patient demonstrated a clinically meaningful reduction in ISI (≥6-point reduction). Some individuals reported reductions in nightmares and improvements in daytime alertness. CONCLUSIONS: In this case series, VSS use was associated with clinically meaningful reductions in chronic insomnia severity in veterans with chronic TBI.

背景：2 型糖尿病和帕金森病 (PD) 具有共同的潜在途径，包括胰岛素抵抗和神经炎症。虽然胰高血糖素样肽 1 受体激动剂 (GLP-1 RA) 在临床前模型中显示出神经保护作用，但临床试验却产生了相互矛盾的结果。这项荟萃分析系统评估了 GLP-1 RA 在 PD 中的疗效和安全性，特别区分了症状缓解和潜在的疾病缓解。方法：我们检索了截至 2025 年 11 月的 PubMed、Scopus、Web of Science、Cochrane Library 和 Embase，查找 GLP-1 RA 治疗特发性 PD 的随机、双盲、安慰剂对照试验。主要运动结果，即运动障碍协会赞助的统一帕金森病评定量表 (MDS-UPDRS) 第 III 部分（运动检查）修订版，使用随机效应模型进行分析，并按“开”与“关”药物状态严格分层。结果：我们纳入了包含 667 名患者的四项高质量试验。 GLP-1 RA 在停药状态（平均差 [MD] - 0.69；95% 置信区间 [CI] - 2.81 至 1.43；p = 0.52）或停药状态（MD - 0.86；95% CI - 3.35 至 1.63；p = 0.50）下均未能显着改善运动功能。此外，对于非运动症状、认知或生活质量没有出现任何有意义的益处。相反，治疗显着增加胃肠道不良事件，包括恶心（风险比[RR] = 2.48）、呕吐（RR = 4.53）和临床上体重减轻（RR = 3.32）。结论：综合最新的 3 期数据，目前的 GLP-1 RA 既不能为更广泛的 PD 人群提供疾病缓解或症状性运动益处。鉴于明显的减肥风险，日常使用它们是没有根据的。未来的试验必须将重点转向生物富集的亚组或新一代肠促胰岛素类似物。

英文摘要

BACKGROUND: Type 2 diabetes and Parkinson's disease (PD) share underlying pathways, including insulin resistance and neuroinflammation. While glucagon-like peptide-1 receptor agonists (GLP-1 RAs) show neuroprotective promise in preclinical models, clinical trials have produced conflicting results. This meta-analysis systematically evaluates the efficacy and safety of GLP-1 RAs in PD, specifically distinguishing between symptomatic relief and potential disease modification. METHODS: We searched PubMed, Scopus, Web of Science, Cochrane Library, and Embase through November 2025 for randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled trials of GLP-1 RAs in idiopathic PD. The primary motor outcome, the Movement Disorder Society-Sponsored Revision of the Unified Parkinson's Disease Rating Scale (MDS-UPDRS) Part III (motor examination), was analyzed using a random-effects model and strictly stratified by "ON" versus "OFF" medication states. RESULTS: We included four high-quality trials comprising 667 patients. GLP-1 RAs failed to significantly improve motor function in either the OFF-medication state (mean difference [MD] - 0.69; 95% confidence interval [CI] - 2.81 to 1.43; p = 0.52) or ON-medication state (MD - 0.86; 95% CI - 3.35 to 1.63; p = 0.50). Furthermore, no meaningful benefits emerged for non-motor symptoms, cognition, or quality of life. Conversely, treatment significantly increased gastrointestinal adverse events, including nausea (risk ratio [RR] = 2.48), vomiting (RR = 4.53), and clinically concerning weight loss (RR = 3.32). CONCLUSIONS: Synthesizing the latest phase 3 data, current GLP-1 RAs offer neither disease-modifying nor symptomatic motor benefits for the broader PD population. Given the pronounced risk of weight loss, their routine use is unwarranted. Future trials must shift focus toward biologically enriched subgroups or newer-generation incretin analogs.

BACKGROUND: Hereditary cerebellar ataxias (HCA) encompass a spectrum of pathological conditions affecting the cerebellum. Currently, there is growing interest in the potential role of mesenchymal stem cells (MSCs) as an investigational therapeutic approach for this condition. Hence, the objective of this single-center, open-label, phase I/IIa clinical trial was to assess the safety and exploratory clinical and biomarker changes following a single intrathecal injection of autologous bone marrow-derived mesenchymal stem cells (BM-MSCs) in HCA. 目的：自体造血干细胞移植 (aHSCT) 仍然是符合条件的多发性骨髓瘤 (MM) 患者的标准治疗方法，成功动员足够的 CD34⁺ 细胞至关重要。本研究评估了单独使用 G-CSF 与 G-CSF 联合不同剂量环磷酰胺进行外周血干细胞动员的有效性和安全性。方法：在这项回顾性、单中心研究中，对 166 名接受首次活动的 MM 患者进行了分析。患者分组如下：单独使用 G-CSF（第 1 组）、G-CSF 加环磷酰胺 1-2 g/m2（第 2 组）和 G-CSF 加环磷酰胺 3-4 g/m2（第 3 组）。成功动员定义为 ≥ 2 × 10⁶ CD34⁺ 细胞/kg。结果包括动员成功、CD34⁺ 产量、血浆分离术要求和毒性。结果：第 1、2 和 3 组的动员成功率分别为 60.6%、84.8% 和 97.6%（p < 0.001）。 CD34⁺细胞产量随着环磷酰胺剂量的增加而增加，其中第3组的产量最高（p < 0.001）。多变量分析表明，环磷酰胺显着提高了动员成功率（第 2 组：OR 4.57，p = 0.007；第 3 组：OR 21.94，p = 0.005）。高剂量组需要的血浆分离术次数较少。发热性中性粒细胞减少症或血小板输注率没有观察到显着差异。结论：在 G-CSF 中添加环磷酰胺可显着提高 MM 患者的动员成功率和 CD34⁺ 产量。较高的环磷酰胺剂量可提供优异的疗效而不增加毒性，支持化疗作为选定患者的有效且安全的策略。

英文摘要

OBJECTIVE: Autologous hematopoietic stem cell transplantation (aHSCT) remains a standard treatment for eligible patients with multiple myeloma (MM), and successful mobilization of adequate CD34⁺ cells is critical. This study evaluated the efficacy and safety of G-CSF alone versus G-CSF combined with different doses of cyclophosphamide for peripheral blood stem cell mobilization. METHODS: In this retrospective, single-center study, 166 patients with MM undergoing first-time mobilization were analyzed. Patients were grouped as follows: G-CSF alone (Group 1), G-CSF plus cyclophosphamide 1-2 g/m² (Group 2), and G-CSF plus cyclophosphamide 3-4 g/m² (Group 3). Successful mobilization was defined as ≥ 2 × 10⁶ CD34⁺ cells/kg. Outcomes included mobilization success, CD34⁺ yield, apheresis requirements, and toxicity. RESULTS: Mobilization success rates were 60.6%, 84.8%, and 97.6% in Groups 1, 2, and 3, respectively (p < 0.001). CD34⁺ cell yield increased with higher cyclophosphamide doses, with the highest yield in Group 3 (p < 0.001). Multivariate analysis showed that cyclophosphamide significantly improved mobilization success (Group 2: OR 4.57, p = 0.007; Group 3: OR 21.94, p = 0.005). Fewer apheresis sessions were required in higher-dose groups. No significant differences were observed in febrile neutropenia or platelet transfusion rates. CONCLUSION: Adding cyclophosphamide to G-CSF significantly improves mobilization success and CD34⁺ yield in MM patients. Higher cyclophosphamide doses provide superior efficacy without increased toxicity, supporting chemo-mobilization as an effective and safe strategy in selected patients.

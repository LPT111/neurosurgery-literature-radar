Alkaline loading of extracellular vesicles produced from human neural stem cell-derived neurospheres enables CNS drug delivery.

PMID: 42315580 ｜ DOI: 10.1038/s41598-026-58290-8

中文摘要

血脑屏障和血肿瘤屏障（分别为 BBB 和 BTB）代表了治疗中枢神经系统疾病（例如脑癌和神经退行性疾病）的药物输送的重大障碍。细胞外囊泡（EV）或外泌体已成为中枢神经系统疾病的新药物递送载体，因为它们可以穿透 BBB/BTB 并且比脂质体载体的免疫原性较低。源自人类神经干细胞 (hNSC) 的 EV 比其他 EV 来源具有更多优势，因为它们增强了神经细胞的归巢能力，并且在啮齿动物模型中显示出治疗中风和创伤性脑损伤的功效。然而，利用 hNSC 的 EV 进行药物输送在很大程度上仍未得到探索，部分原因是与传统细胞系相比，制造能力存在困难。在这里，我们报告了用于 EV 生产和药物输送的 hNSC 悬浮神经球系统的开发。作为概念证明，使用新型高效碱性被动加载方法将阿霉素加载到 hNSC-EV 中，并显示其在体外可有效诱导神经胶质瘤细胞的细胞毒性，并且在体内表现出比单独使用阿霉素更高的血脑屏障外显率。这些研究表明，hNSC-EV 负载的阿霉素具有治疗胶质母细胞瘤等脑癌的潜力，同时也将 hNSC-EV 确立为中枢神经系统疾病的药物输送载体。

英文摘要

The blood brain barrier and blood tumor barrier (BBB and BTB, respectively) represent significant obstacles for the delivery of drugs to treat diseases of the central nervous system, such as brain cancers and neurodegenerative diseases. Extracellular vesicles (EVs) or exosomes have emerged as a new drug delivery vehicle for CNS diseases as they may penetrate the BBB/BTB and are less immunogenic than liposomal carriers. EVs derived from human neural stem cells (hNSC) provide additional benefits over other EV sources due to their increased homing capability to neural cells and demonstrated efficacy for treating stroke and traumatic brain injury in rodent models. However, the utilization of EVs from hNSC for drug delivery remains largely unexplored, due in part to difficulties in manufacturing capacity compared to traditional cell lines. Here, we report the development of a hNSC suspension neurosphere system for EV production and drug delivery. As proof of concept, doxorubicin was loaded into hNSC-EV, using a novel, high-efficiency alkaline passive loading method, and shown to be effective at inducing cytotoxicity in glioma cells in vitro and exhibiting higher BBB penetrance than doxorubicin-alone in vivo. These studies demonstrate the potential for hNSC-EV loaded doxorubicin as a therapeutic treatment for brain cancers such as glioblastoma, while also establishing hNSC-EVs as a drug-delivery vehicle for CNS diseases.

中文摘要

大脑中常驻的巨噬细胞小胶质细胞对免疫反应做出反应并保持中枢神经系统的稳态。小胶质细胞在神经炎症和神经退行性疾病中发挥着关键作用。小胶质细胞反应与神经退行性疾病、自身免疫性疾病和癌症相关，因此需要严格调控。转移 RNA 衍生片段 (tRF) 是由转移 RNA 裂解产生的小型非编码调节 RNA 分子。 tRF 最近已成为各种生物过程中基因表达和细胞功能的关键调节因子之一。了解小胶质细胞中因 LPS 暴露而改变的 tRF 含量可能有助于深入了解疾病发病机制，其中神经炎症在其中发挥作用。我们使用新一代测序来确定 LPS 诱导的小胶质细胞的三种不同组分（裂解物、细胞外囊泡和无细胞外囊泡的上清液）的 tRF 谱。 LPS 刺激后，这些组分中总共有 345 个 tRF 存在差异表达，其中包括所有三个区室共有的 13 个 DE-tRF。对这 13 个 tRF 的基因本体分析表明它们可能参与小胶质细胞的炎症反应。在 13 个持续失调的 DE-tRF 中，选择 tDR-1:33-Glu-CTC-1-M2 进行初步候选水平评估，基于其在我们的 NGS 数据中所有三个片段中一致的差异表达以及之前将该片段与神经炎症背景联系起来的报告。在 LPS 诱导的小胶质细胞的裂解物样品中进一步证实了 tDR-1:33-Glu-CTC-1-M2 表达模式的增加RT-qPCR。抑制 tDR-1:33-Glu-CTC-1-M2 可减弱小胶质细胞中 LPS 诱导的细胞因子表达和释放，支持该候选 tRF 与该模型中炎症读数之间的关联。

英文摘要

The resident macrophages of the brain, microglia, react to immunological responses and preserve homeostasis in the central nervous system. Microglia play a critical role in neuroinflammation, which occurs in neurological and neurodegenerative diseases. Microglial responses are associated with neurodegenerative diseases, autoimmune diseases, and cancer, and therefore require strict regulation. Transfer RNA-derived fragments (tRFs) are small non-coding regulatory RNA molecules generated from the cleavage of transfer RNAs. tRFs have recently emerged as one of the key regulators of gene expression and cellular function in various biological processes. Understanding the content of tRFs altered in microglia due to LPS exposure may provide insights into the mechanisms involved in disease pathogenesis, where neuroinflammation plays a role. We used next-generation sequencing to determine tRF profiles of three different fractions (lysate, extracellular vesicles, and extracellular vesicles-free supernatant) of LPS-induced microglial cells. A total of 345 tRFs were differentially expressed across these fractions after LPS stimulation, including 13 DE-tRFs shared by all three compartments. Gene Ontology analysis of these 13 tRFs suggests their potential involvement in the inflammatory responses of microglia. Among the 13 consistently dysregulated DE-tRFs, tDR-1:33-Glu-CTC-1-M2 was selected for preliminary candidate-level assessment based on its consistent differential expression across all three fractions in our NGS data and previous reports linking this fragment to neuroinflammatory contexts.Increased expression pattern of tDR-1:33-Glu-CTC-1-M2 was further confirmed in lysate samples of LPS-induced microglial cells via RT-qPCR. Inhibition of tDR-1:33-Glu-CTC-1-M2 attenuated LPS-induced cytokine expression and release in microglia, supporting an association between this candidate tRF and inflammatory readouts in this model.

中文摘要

RNA m6A 修饰的失调与阿尔茨海默病 (AD) 有关，但其分子机制仍不清楚。在这里，我们确定了大脑中线粒体编码的信使 RNA (mt-mRNA) 上存在 m6A，其水平升高与淀粉样蛋白 -β (Aβ) 沉积相关。在生理条件下，胞质 m6A 修饰的 mt-Nd4 被 m6A 读取蛋白 YTHDF2 识别并降解，从而防止神经元中 RIG-I-MAVS 先天免疫通路的异常激活。在 Aβ 相关的病理条件下，神经元中 YTHDF2 的表达显着下调，导致 m6A 修饰的 mt-Nd4 在细胞质中积累。这种积累会触发 RIG-I-MAVS 激活和 I 型干扰素 (IFN) 反应。然后，神经元衍生的 IFN-β 通过旁分泌机制激活周围的小胶质细胞，从而放大神经炎症。此外，神经元 Ythdf2 缺陷会加剧 Aβ 相关的神经炎症和认知能力下降。总之，这些发现揭示了一个先前未被认识的 m6A/YTHDF2 依赖性调节轴，该轴将线粒体 RNA 代谢与 Aβ 病理学中的先天免疫激活和神经炎症联系起来。

英文摘要

Dysregulation of RNA m6A modification has been implicated in Alzheimer's disease (AD), but the molecular mechanisms remain largely unclear. Here, we identified the presence of m6A on mitochondria-encoded messenger RNAs (mt-mRNAs) in the brain, with elevated levels correlated with amyloid-β (Aβ) deposition. Under physiological conditions, cytosolic m6A-modified mt-Nd4 is recognized and degraded by the m6A reader protein YTHDF2, thereby preventing aberrant activation of the RIG-I-MAVS innate immune pathway in neurons. Under Aβ-associated pathological conditions, YTHDF2 expression is markedly down-regulated in neurons, leading to the accumulation of m6A-modified mt-Nd4 in the cytosol. This accumulation triggers RIG-I-MAVS activation and type I interferon (IFN) responses. Neuron-derived IFN-β then amplifies neuroinflammation by activating surrounding microglia through a paracrine mechanism. Furthermore, neuronal Ythdf2 deficiency exacerbates Aβ-associated neuroinflammation and cognitive decline. Together, these findings reveal a previously unrecognized m6A/YTHDF2-dependent regulatory axis that links mitochondrial RNA metabolism to innate immune activation and neuroinflammation in Aβ pathology.

中文摘要

创伤性脑损伤（TBI）引起的凝血障碍与患者的不良预后相关，但其进展仍知之甚少。我们纵向采集TBI患者伤后24 h、3 d、7 d的外周静脉血样本，通过实验室检测对样本进行常规血液和常规凝血试验、血栓弹力图、凝血因子活性、血小板活性和细胞外囊泡水平进行分析。通过检测凝血纤溶的纵向变化，我们发现TBI患者在受伤后24小时内处于纤溶亢进状态，但在受伤后第7天转变为高凝状态。并且，从损伤后24小时到7天，细胞外囊泡的浓度持续增加。我们发现了 TBI 引起的凝血病的早期变化，即 TBI 患者在受伤后 24 小时至 7 天内从纤溶亢进状态转变为高凝状态。

英文摘要

Traumatic brain injury (TBI)-induced coagulopathy is associated with poor outcomes of patients, but its progression remains poorly understood. We longitudinally collected peripheral venous blood samples from patients with TBI 24 h, 3 days, and 7 days after injury, and analyzed the samples for conventional blood and conventional coagulation tests, thromboelastography, coagulation factor activity, platelet activity, and levels of extracellular vesicles through laboratory tests. By detecting longitudinal changes in the coagulation-fibrinolysis, we found that TBI patients were in a state of hyperfibrinolysis within 24 h after injury, but transitioned to a hypercoagulable state on the 7th day after injury. And, the concentrations of extracellular vesicles continued to increase from 24 h to 7 days after injury. We identified the early changes in TBI-induced coagulopathy, namely that TBI patients undergo a transition from a hyperfibrinolytic state to a hypercoagulable state within 24 h to 7 days post-injury.

中文摘要

需要新的生物标志物来改善特发性正常压力脑积水（iNPH）的临床特征，iNPH 是老年人认知和功能下降的潜在可逆原因。 Tap Test (TT) 通常用于支持有关脑室腹腔分流术 (VPS) 手术的临床决策；然而，治疗反应的客观标志仍然有限。本研究旨在调查听觉脑干反应 (ABR) 和皮质听觉诱发电位 (CAEP) 测量是否与 TT 的临床反应以及疑似 iNPH 的老年人的纵向临床结果相关。对 55 名老年人（平均年龄：75 ± 7.04 岁）在腰椎穿刺前后以及随访期间（3-12 个月）进行了评估。参与者被分为三个临床结果组：没有 VPS 的 TT 阴性组、没有 VPS 的 TT 阳性组以及接受 VPS 的组。临床评估包括步态和神经心理学测试，而神经生理学评估包括ABR和CAEP。鉴于系统听力学评估对认知和功能结果的潜在影响，先前未诊断的听力损失的高患病率凸显了系统听力学评估在该人群中的重要性。 ABR 异常很常见，但与临床变化无关。 CAEP 并未反映 TT 期间对腰椎穿刺的反应。然而，较短的 P1 潜伏期与更好的步态表现相关，并且 VPS 后 P2 潜伏期的减少可能反映了皮质处理的改善。 CAEP 测量，特别是 P1 和 P2 潜伏期，是监测 iNPH 老年人术后改善的潜在候选标志物；然而，这需要在未来的研究中进一步验证。

英文摘要

New biomarkers are needed to improve the clinical profile of idiopathic normal pressure hydrocephalus (iNPH), a potentially reversible cause of cognitive and functional decline in older adults. The Tap Test (TT) is commonly used to support clinical decision-making regarding ventriculoperitoneal shunt (VPS) surgery; however, objective markers of treatment response remain limited. This study aimed to investigate whether auditory brainstem response (ABR) and cortical auditory evoked potentials (CAEP) measures are associated with clinical response to the TT and with longitudinal clinical outcomes in older adults with suspected iNPH. Fifty-five older adults (mean age: 75 ± 7.04 years) were evaluated before and after lumbar puncture and during follow-up (3-12 months). Participants were categorized into three clinical outcome groups: TT-negative without VPS, TT-positive without VPS, and those who underwent VPS. Clinical assessment included gait and neuropsychological testing, while neurophysiological evaluation comprised ABR and CAEP. A high prevalence of previously undiagnosed hearing loss highlighted the importance of systematic audiological assessment in this population, given its potential impact on cognition and functional outcomes. ABR abnormalities were frequent but not associated with clinical changes. CAEP did not reflect response to lumbar puncture during the TT.
